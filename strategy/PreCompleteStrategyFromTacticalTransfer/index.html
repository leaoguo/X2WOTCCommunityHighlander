PreCompleteStrategyFromTacticalTransfer

Tracking Issue: #785

There are no events that trigger before the mission rewards and several other critical functions are processed. This event gives a way for mods to change several aspects in the transition from tactical to strategy.

EventID: PreCompleteStrategyFromTacticalTransfer
EventData: None
EventSource: None
NewGameState: None
